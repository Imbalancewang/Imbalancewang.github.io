I.About Markdown

We can learn from the introduction that the Markdown is a light weight language. This language focuses on the simple grammar instead of the complex composing, which makes us concentrate on writting itself rather than optimizing the structure. The merits of Markdown

  • Focus on your content instead of composing.
  • Easily export files with the format of HTML, PDF and md.
  • Have a simple grammar and easy to learn.
